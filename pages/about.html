Importance of food and nutrition

Although eating and being nourished are two factors that are interconnected, it is not mandatory that both are always present.

Eating is a process that people carry out when eating food, while nutrition is an involuntary process where the ingested food is converted into nutrients, which is why it is important to have a good diet.

To achieve good nutrition, it is important to focus on the amount of calories and nutrients each patient requires. You also need to know the eating habits you already have, as well as the hours you drive and your food preferences and intolerances. Knowing the diseases already present is also important to achieve healthy nutrition.

Dentro de las funciones que encontrarán en un nutricionista están:

  • Development of an individualized feeding program.
  • Contribute to the creation of good eating habits.
  • Advise on questions related to food.
  • Work to prevent situations that may put the patient's health at risk due to a poor diet.
